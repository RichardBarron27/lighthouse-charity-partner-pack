# About Red Specter Security Research

## Who We Are

Red Specter Security Research is a UK-based cybersecurity company specialising in AI safety and child protection technology. We build tools that make AI systems safer for everyone.

Our tagline is **"Innovation Beyond Belief"** — and we mean it. We believe security tools should be powerful, practical, and accessible.

---

## What We Do

### AI Shield

Our flagship product is **AI Shield**, a 69-module framework that secures autonomous AI agents. It protects against prompt injection, data exfiltration, privilege escalation, and dozens of other threats that emerge when AI systems operate independently.

AI Shield scores full marks against every major AI security framework:

- OWASP LLM Top 10: 10/10
- OWASP Agentic Security: 10/10
- EU AI Act: 7/7
- UK AISI: 13/13

It is used by organisations that deploy AI agents and need to ensure those agents behave safely, securely, and within defined boundaries.

### Guardian Chatbot Monitor

**Guardian Chatbot Monitor** is a child safety platform that monitors AI chatbot conversations across 12 platforms. It detects grooming patterns, sextortion, self-harm language, and other risks using 450 detection patterns across 23 risk categories.

Guardian is designed for schools, charities, and parents who want to understand what is happening when young people talk to AI chatbots — and to be alerted when conversations become concerning.

### Lighthouse — "Still Here"

**Lighthouse** is our free, open-source crisis support extension. Unlike Guardian (which reports to a dashboard), Lighthouse works entirely locally. It shows helpline numbers directly to the person who needs them, without alerting anyone else. No servers, no accounts, no data collection.

Lighthouse is our contribution to public safety. It is free for everyone, forever.

### Purple Team Defensive Suite

We also maintain a suite of open-source defensive security tools used by security professionals worldwide, including breach monitors, ransomware canaries, deepfake detectors, and AI firewall systems.

## Our Approach to Lighthouse

We built Lighthouse because AI chatbots are now a primary companion for millions of vulnerable people — young and old. When someone tells a chatbot they want to end their life, the chatbot may respond helpfully, but it cannot pick up a phone. It cannot call the Samaritans. It cannot sit with someone in their darkest moment.

Lighthouse bridges that gap. It puts real phone numbers in front of real people at the moment they need them most.

We chose to make it:

- **Free and open source** — because this should not be a product
- **Completely private** — because vulnerable people deserve absolute trust
- **Simple to install** — because the people who distribute it are charity workers, not developers
- **Gentle in tone** — because the last thing someone in crisis needs is a clinical alert
