# Privacy Statement

## The Short Version

Lighthouse collects **no data whatsoever**. Nothing ever leaves your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. We cannot see anything you type, and we do not want to.

---

## How Lighthouse Works (In Plain English)

When you visit a supported AI chatbot website, Lighthouse reads the text on the page — just like your eyes do. It checks the words against a list of patterns that may indicate distress (such as self-harm language or expressions of loneliness).

If it finds a match, it shows a small panel with helpline numbers.

Then it **throws away the text**. Immediately. It is not saved anywhere. Not on your computer, not on a server, not in a file, not in a log. Nowhere.

## What Is Stored

The only thing Lighthouse ever saves to your device is a single timestamp — when you click "Remind me later", it saves the time the snooze expires (so it knows when to start showing helplines again). That is it. One number.

## What Is NOT Stored

- Your messages — never saved, never logged
- Your chatbot conversations — never recorded
- Your browsing history — never tracked
- Your identity — never collected
- Your location — never requested
- Cookies — none created
- Analytics or telemetry — none, ever

## Network Activity

Lighthouse makes **zero network requests**. It does not connect to any server, API, or external service. You can verify this yourself by opening your browser's Developer Tools (F12), going to the Network tab, and watching. There will be no requests from Lighthouse. Not one.

## No Accounts

There is nothing to sign up for. No email address, no username, no password. Install the extension and it works.

## No Third-Party Services

Lighthouse does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Mixpanel, Sentry, or any other third-party service. There are no tracking scripts, no advertising libraries, and no data-sharing arrangements with anyone.

## Open Source

Every line of code is available for inspection. If you want to verify any of the claims in this document, you can read the source code yourself. It is written in plain JavaScript with no build step, no obfuscation, and no minification.

## Children and Vulnerable People

Lighthouse is designed for use by vulnerable people, including children. We take that responsibility seriously. The absolute minimum data footprint — which is zero — reflects our belief that the best way to protect privacy is to never collect data in the first place.

## Data Protection

Because Lighthouse collects no personal data, it does not fall within the scope of UK GDPR or the Data Protection Act 2018. There is nothing to process, nothing to store, and nothing to delete. If you have questions about this, contact us at the address below.
